{% endblock %} {% block steps %}

  1. Get a free energy assessment from your current energy utility or a non-profit to find out where green upgrades would make the biggest impact.

  2. Pursuing green strategies can expedite your permit process through the Green Permit program.

  3. See if you’re eligible for rebates or installations through the city’s Retrofit Chicago program. Free efficiency items can include smart water meters, and rain barrels.

  4. Owners of buildings with 1-4 residential units may be eligible for Energy Efficiency Loan Options offered by the Chicago Community Loan Fund.
