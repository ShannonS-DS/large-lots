The Basics

All large lot owners are required to fence their lot (if the lot is not adjacent to property they already own) with a noncombustible screen fence while the lot remains vacant. Noncombustible materials will not ignite at high temperatures. Fences must be constructed so that at least 50% of the fence has openings that can be seen through, like in a lattice pattern. Fences taller than 5ft require an easy permit.
